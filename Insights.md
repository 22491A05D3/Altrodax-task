# Business Insights

## 1. Data Analytics Track Shows Strong Engagement
Students enrolled in the Data Analytics track demonstrated higher completion rates compared to other tracks.

## 2. Web Development Track Has Higher Dropout Risk
The Web Development track recorded a fail/dropout rate exceeding 40%, indicating the need for additional mentoring and support.

## 3. IIT Bombay Students Reach Aptitude Milestones Faster
Students from IIT Bombay achieved Level 50 in fewer average days, suggesting stronger preparation and engagement.

## 4. SQL Basics Has Highest Skill Completion Rate
The SQL Basics module achieved the highest completion percentage, making it an effective foundational course.
